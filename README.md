## 📌 Overview

**Hoysala Degree College (HDC)** is a private undergraduate institution established in **2019** under the **Hoysala Academy** — a registered educational society with over two decades of involvement in higher education and vocational training across Karnataka.

Located in the **Nelamangala region of Bengaluru Rural, Karnataka**, HDC is affiliated with **Bangalore University** and is committed to delivering quality, value-based higher education to students from both rural and urban backgrounds.

## 📚 Courses Offered

HDC offers undergraduate programs in **Commerce and Management** streams:

| Program                                       | Duration | Stream                        |
| --------------------------------------------- | -------- | ----------------------------- |
| **B.Com** (Bachelor of Commerce)              | 3 Years  | Accounting & Commerce         |
| **BBA** (Bachelor of Business Administration) | 3 Years  | Business & Management Studies |

> **Total Intake Capacity:** ~200 seats across all programs

### Admission

- Admissions are **merit-based**, following the eligibility criteria and norms of Bangalore University.
- Eligibility: Pass in 10+2 or equivalent from any recognized board.
